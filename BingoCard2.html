Category Descriptions

  • Candy Cane Sighting: Notice someone eating or buying candy canes.
  • Charity Donation Booth: Spot a booth accepting donations for charity.
  • Child Meltdown: You'll know this when you see it.
  • Children's Holiday Wish List: Overhear a child talking about their wish list.
  • Crowded Parking Lot: Witness the struggle to find a parking spot.
  • Decor Overdrive: Find a store with over-the-top holiday decorations.
  • Elves in Action: Spot mall staff dressed as elves or in festive attire.
  • Exhausted Mall Employee: Spot a mall employee who looks tired but is still smiling.
  • Festive Window Display: Admire a creatively decorated store window.
  • Free Samples: Encounter a store giving out free samples.
  • Gift Wrapping Station: Pass by a gift-wrapping service area.
  • Happy Family Moment: See a family taking a photo together.
  • Holiday Greeting Exchange: Witness people exchanging holiday greetings.
  • Holiday Music Overload: Hear a classic Christmas song.
  • Holiday Sale Sign: Find a store advertising a holiday sale.
  • Hot Beverage Comfort: See someone enjoying a hot drink like cocoa or a latte.
  • Impromptu Caroling: Hear or see a group of carolers.
  • Last-Minute Shopper: Observe a shopper who looks hurried and frantic.
  • Matching Family Outfits: Notice a family wearing matching holiday outfits.
  • Mistletoe in Hallway: Find mistletoe hanging in the mall.
  • Overflowing Shopping Bags: Notice someone carrying multiple shopping bags.
  • Pet in Holiday Attire: See a pet dressed up in festive gear.
  • Santa's Workshop: Spot a Santa Claus area with kids lined up.
  • Seasonal Store: Find a pop-up shop or kiosk that's only there for the holidays.
  • Seasonal Treats: See a kiosk or store selling holiday-themed food or drinks.
  • Ugly Christmas Sweater: See someone wearing an ugly Christmas sweater.
